Your single living source of truth. Anseru connects to where your knowledge already lives. Sync Google Drive, SharePoint, Teams, Jira, Slack, and Dropbox or upload your internal documents directly. Your answer library builds itself and stays current automatically.

  • Indexes past RFPs, security docs, and compliance content automatically
  • Integrates with your existing storage and communication systems
  • Improves continuously as your team uses and approves new answers

Upload RFPs & Security Questionnaires

Extract precise constraints with pinpoint accuracy

Drop in any document in any format. Anseru reads the full document, identifies every requirement, and maps each question to your verified knowledge base, in seconds.

  • Accepts PDF, DOCX, XLSX documents.
  • Automatically identifies and classifies every question and sub-requirement
  • Maps each item to your verified answer library before you begin

Generate AI Draft Responses

Produce high-quality answers tied to real company data

Anseru generates a full response draft using your verified documentation and past approved answers. Every answer is traceable to a real source, so drafts arrive ready for review, not ready to be rewritten from scratch.

  • Drafts complete responses from your verified internal knowledge base
  • Flags low-confidence answers for SME review before submission
  • Saves hours of manual writing and cross-referencing per deal

SME Review & Approval

Collaborate securely with robust human-in-the-loop workflows

Anseru focuses your experts on what only they can answer. Standard questions arrive pre-drafted and ready to approve. Novel, flagged, and high-risk items are surfaced for review before anything leaves the building.

  • Routes only flagged and novel questions to subject matter experts
  • Full audit trail of every review, edit, and approval decision
  • Every submission is clean, compliant, and signed off before it goes out

FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Anseru?
+

Anseru is a deal intelligence platform designed for enterprise sales teams. It helps companies respond to RFPs, security questionnaires, and compliance requests by turning past deal knowledge into structured, reusable intelligence.

How is Anseru different from traditional RFP tools?
+

Most RFP tools focus on storing answers or generating responses. Anseru focuses on understanding the deal context — including industry, compliance requirements, and prior responses — so teams don't have to start from scratch each time.

Who is Anseru built for?
+

Anseru is built for teams involved in complex enterprise deals, including solutions engineering, security and compliance, and revenue operations. These teams typically collaborate to respond to procurement and due diligence requests.

How quickly can we start seeing value?
+

Teams typically see value within their first few deals. As more RFPs or security questionnaires are processed, Anseru begins reusing existing knowledge and reduces manual effort over time.

How accurate are the responses generated by Anseru?
+

Anseru combines AI-generated responses with validation layers such as source references, review workflows, and internal standards. This ensures responses are reliable, auditable, and aligned with company guidelines.

How does Anseru improve over time?
+

Anseru learns from every interaction, edit, approval, and past response captured across deals. This allows the system to continuously improve accuracy and relevance over time.

How does Anseru integrate with our existing tools?
+

Anseru is designed to work alongside your existing systems, including CRM platforms, document repositories, and collaboration tools. It pulls in relevant knowledge while allowing teams to continue working within familiar workflows.

How do you ensure our data is secure?
+

Anseru is built with enterprise-grade security in mind, including strict data isolation, access controls, and auditability. Customer data is handled within secure environments and protected according to compliance standards.

Do you use our data to train shared AI models?
+

No. Customer data is not used to train shared or public models. Each organization's data remains isolated and is used only to improve its own responses and intelligence within the platform.
